About Us

Gold jewelry is a popular choice for its natural beauty and radiance. It is made by mixing pure gold with other metal elements while in the molten state to create alloys with different properties such as strength and various colors1. The most common type of gold alloy is yellow in color and contains silver and zinc2. The resulting material is more durable for long-lasting jewelry that is easy to care for1. Gold filled jewelry consists of a thick layer of gold over a base metal like jeweler’s Brass, and the gold must represent at least 5% of the item’s total weight by law13.
